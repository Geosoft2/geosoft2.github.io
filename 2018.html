About the course

During the winter semester 2018/19 the bachelor students at the Institute for Geoinformatics (ifgi) worked on geospatial research data in data repositories and catalogs. The course Geosoft II introduces students to advanced application development (web services, user interfaces) for geospatial data. This year the students had to work with geospatial metadata, research metadata, metadata catalogs, and data repositories to advance the discovery of research datasets beyond text-based search. You can download the full project requirements document.
